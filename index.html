The Story

Pickforks was created because I thought that the current way that people find interior designers is not the optimal way. With so many options, it's a challenge for both to find the right match. And most of the time, we settle on the one that's available. Or settle because time runs out on us. Pickforks was made because its a better way for interior designers to find homeowners, and for homeowners to find them.
